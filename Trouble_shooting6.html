Laptop Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • Slow performance: Close unnecessary programs, check for malware, and consider upgrading RAM.

  • Screen not turning on: Try a hard reset by removing the battery and holding the power button for 10-20 seconds.

  • Battery not charging: Check the power adapter and connections. Try a different outlet.

  • Overheating: Ensure vents are not blocked. Clean dust from fans.

Desktop Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • No power: Check power supply connections and ensure the power outlet is functional.

  • Monitor no signal/display: Check cable connections, try a different monitor, or test the graphics card.

  • Unresponsive mouse/keyboard: Check USB connections, replace batteries for wireless devices, or try different ports.

  • Slow performance: Remove unnecessary startup programs and consider disk cleanup or defragmentation.

Printer Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • Fixed Blinking Ink Light: Make sure your printer is turned off, press and hold cancel button while you turn on the printer for ten seconds and after ten seconds release and press again cancel button.

  • Incorrect Color or Missing Colors: Replace or refill cartridges, perform a nozzle check or head cleaning, and ensure the printer settings match the desired print color.

  • Poor Print Quality: Run the printer's cleaning function to clear clogged printheads. For laser printers, clean the drum or replace the toner.

General Computer Troubleshooting

Steps to Follow:

  1. Restart the computer.
  2. Check for software updates.
  3. Run a virus/malware scan.
  4. Check for hardware issues (e.g., loose cables, damaged components).
  5. Consult the manufacturer's website for specific troubleshooting guides.
